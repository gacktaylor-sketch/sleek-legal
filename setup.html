Using Sleek with OpenRouter

How to connect your OpenRouter account or API key

What Sleek is

Sleek is a native iOS client for OpenRouter. You bring your own OpenRouter key, and Sleek lets you chat with every model OpenRouter offers from a fast, native app — with SillyTavern character-card import and history stored only on your device. Sleek has no account and no servers of its own; it talks directly to OpenRouter using your key.

Getting connected

There are two ways to give Sleek an OpenRouter key. Either works — pick one.

Option 1 — Connect your OpenRouter account

  1. Open Sleek and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Connect OpenRouter.
  3. A secure web sheet opens on openrouter.ai. Sign in (or create a free OpenRouter account if you don't have one) and approve access.
  4. OpenRouter mints a key for Sleek automatically and hands you back to the app. You're ready to chat.

Option 2 — Paste an API key

  1. Sign in at openrouter.ai/keys and create a new API key.
  2. Copy the key (it begins with sk-or-).
  3. In Sleek, go to Settings → Manage API Keys and paste it in.

Adding credit

Model usage is billed by OpenRouter to your own OpenRouter account, at OpenRouter's own rates — Sleek adds no markup and takes no payment. Add credit on your OpenRouter credits page. Many models also have free tiers you can use right away.

Where your key lives

Your key is stored only on your device and is sent only to OpenRouter's API to make the requests you ask for. It never goes to us — Sleek has no servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
