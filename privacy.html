Sleek — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

The short version

Sleek has no servers and no account system. We do not collect, receive, or store your data. Your API key, conversations, and settings live on your device. When you send a message, it goes directly from your device to OpenRouter using your own key — it never passes through us. We run no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

1. Who we are

This policy describes how the Sleek mobile app (the “App”) handles information. Sleek is an independent, “bring-your-own-key” client for OpenRouter. It is not affiliated with OpenRouter or any model provider.

2. Information stored on your device

The following stay on your device and are not transmitted to us:

  • Your OpenRouter API key (kept in your device’s secure storage).
  • Your conversations, messages, and any attached images or files.
  • Your settings, presets, and memory entries.

We do not operate servers that receive or store any of this. It remains under your control on your device.

3. Information sent to OpenRouter

To generate a response, the App sends your request — including the text you type and any images, audio, or files you choose to include — directly from your device to OpenRouter, authenticated with your API key. OpenRouter then routes it to the AI model you selected. This transmission is necessary for the App to function.

OpenRouter and the underlying model providers process that content under their own privacy policies and terms, which we do not control. Please review OpenRouter’s privacy policy to understand how they handle your data. We never receive a copy of your prompts or responses.

4. What we do not collect

We do not collect personal information. The App has no user accounts, no login, no analytics or telemetry, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. We do not build profiles, and we do not sell or share data — because we do not receive your data in the first place.

5. Device permissions

The App asks for certain permissions only when you use the related feature, and only to make that feature work:

  • Microphone — for voice chat and dictation. Recorded audio is sent to the AI model you selected (via OpenRouter) to be transcribed or responded to; it is not sent to us.
  • Photos / Camera — so you can attach images to a message, and so the App can save generated images or videos to your library. These are handled on your device and through the operating system.

You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

6. Purchases

The App is free at launch. If in-app purchases are offered in a future version, payments are processed by Apple under Apple’s privacy policy, and subscription status may be managed through a purchase-management provider (RevenueCat). That process uses a non-personal app identifier and your purchase/entitlement status only — it does not include your conversations or prompts. We never receive your payment details.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children. You must be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction) to use it. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Data retention and deletion

Because your data is stored only on your device, you control it entirely. You can clear your conversations and data from within the App’s settings, and deleting the App removes everything stored on your device. To delete data held by OpenRouter or a model provider, use their respective tools and policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted at this URL.
